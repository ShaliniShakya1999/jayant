Unlock instant financial freedom with Gold Loan Services by Jayant India. Gold is one of the safest and most dependable assets, and we help you convert your gold jewellery into quick, secure financial support for personal, emergency, business, or educational needs across Haryana.

Who can apply for a Gold Loan?

Any individual owning gold jewellery, including salaried persons, self-employed professionals, small business owners, traders, and cooperative society members, can apply for a Gold Loan.

What are the interest rates & tenure?

Gold Loan interest rates range from 10% to 18%. Loan tenure varies between 12 to 24 months with flexible repayment and interest-only payment options available.

What documents are required?

Basic KYC documents such as Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport are required. Gold jewellery (22K / 24K) will be verified for purity and weight at the branch. No additional ownership documents are required.

About Us

Jayant India (JINL)

Jayant India (JINL) is a member-focused financial services organization built on trust, transparency, and responsible innovation. We adopt a technology-enabled approach to simplify financial access and improve the overall member experience, while operating strictly within the framework applicable to a Nidhi Company.

Our philosophy is simpleâ€”finance should be accessible, understandable, and responsible. By leveraging digital processes and structured systems, we aim to make routine financial interactions more efficient, convenient, and member-friendly, without compromising on compliance or ethical standards.

JINL operates on a closed, members-only model, where all financial activities are undertaken exclusively for registered members and in accordance with applicable laws. We do not position ourselves as a bank or a deposit-taking NBFC, and our offerings are designed to support mutual benefit, financial discipline, and long-term stability.

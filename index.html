Our Services

All-in-One Digital Banking, Payments & Loan Solutions

Jayant India empowers businesses, cooperatives, and individuals with a complete suite of connected banking, digital payments, verification APIs, and smart loan solutions—secure, fast, and fully compliant.

Explore Services

Connected Banking

Link all your bank accounts in one dashboard and manage finances with real-time insights, automation, and control.

Utility Payments

Pay electricity, telecom, GST and business bills from one panel with bulk payments, reminders and GST-ready invoices.

UPI Collection

Collect payments instantly using UPI links and QR codes with real-time settlement and seamless integration.

Micro ATM

Convert your outlet into a mini ATM offering cash withdrawals, balance enquiry, and commission-based services.

Instant Payouts

Disburse funds instantly via UPI, IMPS or NEFT with automated processing and complete transaction tracking.

Travel Booking

Book bus and flight tickets in real time and earn commissions— ideal for travel agents and retail businesses.

Aadhaar Banking (AEPS)

Provide AEPS services such as withdrawals and balance checks using Aadhaar and secure biometric authentication.

Verification APIs

Instantly verify PAN, Aadhaar, GST, bank accounts and more with secure, scalable and ready-to-use APIs.

Gold Loan

Quick approvals, attractive interest rates and 100% transparent, secure gold-backed financing.

Mortgage Loan

Long-term financial solutions with flexible repayment, lower interest rates and faster processing.

Loan Against Deposit

Avail instant loans against fixed deposits while continuing to earn FD interest with no premature penalty.
